Island Challenge
Replaces Gyms. Complete Trials on each island, then face the Island Kahuna.

Z-Moves
Powerful one-use moves activated via Z-Crystals and Z-Ring. Each type has one.
🌺
Alolan Forms
Classic Pokémon reimagined with new typings adapted to Alola's unique environment.
🏇
Ride Pokémon
HMs removed. Summon Ride Pokémon via the Ride Pager for field navigation.
🆘
SOS Battles
Wild Pokémon can call for help. Chain allies for shiny hunting or rare abilities.
☀️
Time Difference
Sun is 12 hrs ahead of Moon. Certain Pokémon only appear during Sun's time of day.
🗺️ Region: Alola
Based on the Hawaiian Islands. Four main islands — Melemele, Akala, Ula'ula, and Poni — plus the man-made Aether Paradise. Each island has its own Kahuna and Trial Captains.

T1
Verdant Cavern Trial
Captain Ilima · Normal Type · Melemele Island
Normal
⚔️ Totem Pokémon (Sun)
Totem Gumshoos
Lv 12 · Ally: Yungoos (Lv 9)
Normal
Gumshoos has boosted Sp. Defense from its Totem aura. Use physical Fighting moves.
Best counter: Makuhita (Melemele Meadow), Crabrawler (Berry Fields). Any Lv 13+ Fighting type wins cleanly.
Avoid using Ghost types — Normal is immune to Ghost.
Reward: Normalium Z · TM31 Brick Break from Ilima
T2
Brooklet Hill Trial
Captain Lana · Water Type · Akala Island
Water
⚔️ Totem Pokémon
Totem Wishiwashi
Lv 20 (School Form) · Ally: Alomomola (Lv 18)
Water
Wishiwashi's School Form has enormous HP and Attack. Priority moves to break its HP threshold (25% HP drops it to Solo form).
Best counters: Charjabug (Route 5 Lv14+), Pikachu, Trumbeak with Peck for chip.
Alomomola will Heal Pulse Wishiwashi — take out the ally first if possible.
Reward: Waterium Z · TM39 Rock Tomb from Lana

Wela Volcano Trial
Captain Kiawe · Fire Type · Akala Island
Fire
⚔️ Totem Pokémon
Totem Salazzle
Lv 22 · Allies: Salandit ×2 (Lv 19)
FirePoison

T4
Lush Jungle Trial
Captain Mallow · Grass Type · Akala Island
Grass
⚔️ Totem Pokémon
Totem Lurantis
Lv 24 · Allies: Castform (Lv 22), Comfey (Lv 19)
Grass
Lurantis has huge Attack. Castform may set up Sunny Day boosting Solar Blade — a one-hit KO potential.
Priority: KO Castform first with Ice, Fire, or Poison. Then tackle Lurantis.
Best counters: Salazzle, Salandit, Fletchinder, Trumbeak. Fire, Ice, Bug, Flying all work.

Electric
⚔️ Totem Pokémon
Totem Vikavolt
Lv 29 · Ally: Charjabug (Lv 27)
BugElectric

Captain Acerola · Ghost Type · Ula'ula Island
Ghost
⚔️ Totem Pokémon
Totem Mimikyu
Lv 33 · Allies: Haunter (Lv 31), Jellicent (Lv 31)
GhostFairy
Mimikyu's Disguise ability absorbs the first hit for free — don't waste a Z-Move on it. Use a weak move first to break Disguise.
Mimikyu is immune to Normal, Fighting, and Dragon. Use Ghost, Steel, or Poison.

No Captain — self-guided · Dragon Type · Poni Island
Dragon
⚔️ Totem Pokémon
Totem Kommo-o
Lv 45 · Ally: Scizor (Lv 43) or Hakamo-o
DragonFighting
Kommo-o is the hardest Totem. It boosts Special Defense on entry. Use physical special Dragon counters.
Fairy is immune to Dragon and hits for 2×. Primarina and Ribombee are ideal.

🌟 Starter Recommendations
🔥 Incineroar — Best Overall Starter
Fire/Dark typing covers Ghost (Dark), Fairy (Steel/Poison on team), Ice (Fire). Intimidate ability on hidden is incredible. Best for tackling the Elite Four lineup. Recommended pick for first playthrough.
💧 Primarina — Best for New Players
Water/Fairy handles Hala (Fairy), Olivia (Water), Nanu (Fairy), Kommo-o (Fairy). Very bulky special attacker. Covers the most Grand Trials of any starter.
🌿 Decidueye — Best for Experienced Players
Grass/Ghost has tricky coverage. Spirit Shackle traps opponents. Needs strong team support due to Dark, Fire, Flying, Ice, Rock weaknesses. High skill ceiling reward.
🏆 Battle Tree Team
For the post-game Battle Tree facility. Fully optimised at Lv 50. Comp-adjacent.
Ride Pokémon
Gen VII's replacement for HMs · Pager required
📱 The Ride Pager
In Pokémon Sun, HMs are completely removed. Instead, you call Ride Pokémon via your Ride Pager (a feature of the Rotom Pokédex). You receive calls throughout the story — they don't occupy a party slot and don't need to know moves. Each Ride Pokémon replaces one or more classic HM functions.
